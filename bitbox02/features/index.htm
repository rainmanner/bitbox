BitBox02 features

Level up your security with the following features

Invisible touch sensors

The BitBox02 hardware wallet has capacitive touch sensors to interact with the device.

Tap to navigate and select.

Slide to verify your addresses.

Hold ensures you won't make any accidental transactions.

Easy backup and restore

For peace of mind, backups are made to a microSD card instantly and can be managed from the BitBoxApp. Optionally you can display your 24 recovery words.

OLED display

The large 128 x 64px display lets you verify directly on the BitBox02 what you are about to confirm, including all transaction details before signing.

Mobile ready

Plug directly into your Android phone or computer with USB-C. The BitBox02 comes with an optional USB-C to USB-A adaptor and cable included.

Explore the BitBoxApp

Our supported coins

The BitBox02 supports Bitcoin (BTC), Litecoin (LTC), Cardano (ADA), Ethereum (ETH) and ERC20 tokens. Among them are Chainlink, DAI, USDT and BAT. More coins are planned in the future.

See all supported cryptocurrencies

Secure chip

A secure chip protects against physical tampering. This includes prevention of brute-force attacks and improved protection of your private keys.

Learn more about the BitBox02 security features

Universal second factor (U2F)

Use your BitBox02 as a second factor authentication key for your favourite online accounts, such as email and crypto exchanges.

See which websites support U2F

Bitcoin-only edition available

The BitBox02 Bitcoin-only edition has limited firmware and only supports Bitcoin. Less code means less attack surface which further improves your security when only storing Bitcoin.

Specifications

Connectivity: USB-C

Compatibility: Windows 7 and later, macOS 10.13 and later, Linux, Android

Input: Capacitive touch sensors

Microcontroller: ATSAMD51J20A; 120 Mhz 32-bit Cortex-M4F; True random number generator

Secure chip: ATECC608B; True random number generator (NIST SP 800-90A/B/C)

Display: 128 x 64 px white OLED

Material: Polycarbonate

Size: 54.5 x 25.4 x 9.6 mm including USB-C plug

Weight: Device 12g; with packaging and accessories 160g
